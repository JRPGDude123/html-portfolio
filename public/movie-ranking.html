The Best Movies According to Nick

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Midnight in Paris

Starring Owen Wilson. This movie is about an author who is on vacation with his fiance in Paris. He goes for a walk one night and ends up getting picked up by a cab that takes him to the past.

Bullet Train

Starring Brad Pitt. This movie is jam-packed with action, taking place on a Japanese speed train. It feels like a movie that Quentin Tarentino himself directed.

Catch Me If You Can

A lovely film starring Leonardo DiCaprio and Tom Hanks, in a classic game of cat and mouse.
